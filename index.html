Mintegral compliance checklist

  • ZIP ≤ 5 MB, all assets local (no CDN / XHR)
  • Calls gameReady after assets load

  • Calls gameEnd when interaction ends
  • Full-screen tap uses window.install() (not ExitApi)
  • Mouse + touch clicks work (PC validator)
